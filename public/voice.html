Voice
The Web Wizardry voice is be bright and cheery, but still professional and earnest. We create fun, engaging, and creative products, but we are also serious about our work. This is reflected in the tone of our material. The cheeriness in our written material displays our attitude about design and the user experience, and the element of earnest professionalism communicates that we care about our clients' site's functionality and security alongside the creative appeal of a product. Our mission, to create high quality products that draw the user into the story of the brand is apparent in our brand's voice.
