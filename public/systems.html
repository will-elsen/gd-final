Type System
Header font: Copperplate Medium The copperplate light font is our header font. It conveys both the mystical design perspective we take in our work, and the seriousness with which we regard our security and functionality. Body font: Garamond ATF Micro The garamond ATF Micro font is our body font. Garamond ATF Micro, with the very small serif communicates the attention to detail of the Web Wizardry brand, and also the whimsy of our design. Its fancier than fonts like Helvetica while still being easy on the eyes. The serif, while present and noticeable, does not detract from the readability of the font, but simply adds a touch of the Web Wizardry brand and mission.
