Color System
#91298e/#d4c5e1: Rich Magenta/Thin Violet: Purple should be used when discussing our design philosophy. On any page that describes our emphasis on unique design, rich magenta inside thin violet should appear as accents. these colors highlight the uniqueness and creativity of our designs. #2d2e73/#9b9fcf: Deep Blue/Blueberry: Used when discussing functional aspects of our work. We use deep blue surrounded by blueberry when discussing the functionality of an app or website. These colors convey professionality and calculated reliability, representing our approach to functionality. #fdbf14/#fde189: Sunshine Yellow/Lemon: Sunshine Yellow surrounded by Lemon will be used when referencing the security practices of Web Wizards. On any page or material that discusses our emphasis on security and safety, we use these colors as accents. These colors emphasize safety and security.
